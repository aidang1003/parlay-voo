The mechanism of a prediction market are intentionally structured different than a casino for an important reason.
Our current structure is a more of a casino with a LP for funding.
Instead we should re-architect the system around RFQs which are peer-to-peer oriented parlay markets in the traditional polymarket sense.
Where we are different is that we have a liquidity pool that operates as a more sophisticated market maker ensure that parlays have a seller on the other end of the transaction faster.
We will need to keep the polymarket pulls to get a baseline of what a fair parlay is - especially while volumes are low.
